winter needs

During May and June we may experience the snow and snow fall in ladakh (Temperature +5 to -5) so please bring below mentioned things to protect yourself against cold.

  • Good winter Jacket & winter Pant
  • Rain Jacket & Rain Pant
  • Thermal inner wear (Upper & Lower)
  • Woolen socks extra pair, Woolen gloves , Woolen cap, Woolen Scarf
  • Sleeping bag (If you have) And you can bring anything related to winter survival

documents to carry
  1. driving licence
  2. Rc book original (If the vehicle is not in your name then you cant enter into ladakh. But if it is in yours family members name like: father/mother/own brother/ own sister / then get the no objection letter from them for using their vehicle for this ride) Subject to LADAKH authorities approval*
  3. Comprehensive insurance is mandatory (Copy) (if you have only 3rdparty coverage then I recommend you to avail full cover which may cost you Rs 800 to Rs 2000)
  4. Pollution test certificate which will cost to Rs 80 to Rs 100. (if your vehicle is less than 1 years old then this certificate is not mandatory) Original Adhaar or Passport or Voter id any one proof is mandatory
  5. SIM card – Prepaid SIM works till Manali range only, so buy one postpaid SIM card with 2weeks or one month subscription which will work in ladakh range. (Preferably BSNL & AIRTEL )
  6. If you are a foreigner then please carry all necessary documents with you.

FOR YOUR SAFETY (PLS EXCUSE ME:WITHOUT THIS YOU CANT JOIN IN THIS RIDE )

  • Full face helmet with transparent visor (Please avoid half face helmet and also avoid black tinted visors which will affect your road view during rain and night rides ) 2 side view mirrors are mandatory
  • Riding Jacket & Riding pant (if not at-least use Knee and Elbow guard)
  • Riding boots if not any leather boot
  • Riding glove
  • Rain Jacket and Rain pants
  • Paste 3M reflective stickers on 4 side of your motorcycle For your enhanced safety, if you like to add more you can bring them.
